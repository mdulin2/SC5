Injection City

These are all injection based challenges. Here are a few tips:

  • Look at issues with dynamic strings being used in a given context. Once you understand why using dynamic content is bad in this context, you'll have a good chance to solve it.
  • Copy the command/query/whatever into a text editor. Then, add in your payload into the injected here. This helps with the exploit payload generation.
  • The full command must be valid syntax. Otherwise, it probably won't execute.
  • The data to alter is either a parameter in the URL or a form field on the page. Have fun!
